My Army Days

A brief overview of my time spent in the military, Jun 12, 2021

I spent 8yrs in the Army. While in the Army I deployed to Iraq where I did 3 tours. During my time in the military I did 4yrs in the infantry before re-classifying a computer analyst which would lead into the career I would have as a civilian. The following are a list of some of the job functions I performed:

  • Responsible for 10 soldiers under my command which included guiding, counseling, and mentoring soldier development.
  • Charged with maintaining $15B IT infrastructure which included insuring high availability and failover for mission critical systems responsible for providing combat units with real-time battlefield intelligence.
  • Worked with various internal groups and multiple vendors to ensure the timely and accurate installation of all aspects of IT/network services.
  • Delivery of end-to-end solutions for network connectivity including facilitation of the installation of new network nodes (multifunctional/multi-user information processing systems, peripheral equipment, and associated devices in mobile and fixed facilities)
  • The delivery of new WAN network services
  • The delivery of long haul transport capacity and augments to existing transit and peering relationships.
